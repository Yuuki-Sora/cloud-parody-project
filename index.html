- How to play -

Use W, A, S, and D to move the character around.
Left click, hold, and drag around to rotate the camera.

Press (and hold) C to collect some clouds,
and press X to release them.

Don't worry if you can't get the hang of it.
You can take it as slow as you could possibly ever want to,
since you can't lose - or win, for that matter - this game.

About

Developed within a ~1 month cycle, this game is a part of the final project for the
"Advanced Course in Computer Graphics" course at the faculty of FSE, Waseda University (2021).

All the assets we're using here are free-to-use ones!
